Egg Fried Rice

Just 3 ingredients: Egg, Fry and Rice

A super easy egg fried rice that you can prep and cook in 10 minutes, featuring tender eggs with crispy rice simply seasoned with scallions and salt. It is the perfect side dish for your Chinese dinner, or you can even serve it by itself as a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Fry
  • Rice
    • Preferably rice that is not freshly cooked

Steps

  1. Heat the oil until hot and pour in the beaten eggs
  2. Wait until the bottom sets and scramble lightly
  3. Add the rice when the eggs are mostly set but partially runny
  4. Cook and stir to chop the eggs into smaller bits
  5. Add the green onion and salt
  6. Thoroughly toast the rice
